# Subscription Plan Tiers Matrix

This document outlines the standard commercial subscription tiers, monthly pricing slabs, and feature limits of BhoomiOne.

---

## 📋 Plan Tiers Matrix

### 1. STARTER Plan Slabs
* **Standard Monthly Price**: \$99/month
* **Available Features**: Projects creation, manual plots entries, dashboard logging.
* **Volume Constraints**: Maximum of 3 active Projects, 150 plots limit.
* **Feature Exclusions**: NO CAD DXF imports, NO SVG generators, NO live GIS maps georeferencing.

### 2. GROWTH Plan Slabs
* **Standard Monthly Price**: \$299/month
* **Available Features**: Unlocks CAD parser, custom layers mapper studio, dynamic SVG generators, and automated boundary subdivision calculators.
* **Volume Constraints**: Maximum of 15 active Projects, 2,000 plots limit.

### 3. ENTERPRISE Plan Slabs
* **Standard Monthly Price**: \$899/month
* **Available Features**: Unlocks all premium features (High-precision WGS84 Georeferencing, satellite overlay visualizers, dynamic multi-vendor map adapters).
* **Volume Constraints**: Unlimited Projects, Unlimited Plots.
