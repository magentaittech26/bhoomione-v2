This document lists all active system-wide feature codes registered within the commercial billing engine database.

| **`custom_domains`** | Custom Brand Domain | Tenancy | Redirect DNS to custom white-labeled developer subdomains. |
| **`bulk_crm_export`** | CRM Data Portability | CRM | Export customer profiles and collections log sheets. |
