# Subscription Add-Ons & Pricing Slabs

This document details standalone commercial add-ons available for tenants wanting to unlock premium capacities without changing their core plan.

---

## 🔌 Core Subscription Add-Ons

### 1. GIS Mapping Pack (`gis_maps`)
* **Purpose**: Unlocks high-precision coordinates georeferencing, live satellite layers, and WGS84 GeoJSON exports.
* **Stand-alone Pricing**: \$149/month (Available as add-on for `Starter` and `Growth` plan subscribers).

### 2. DXF CAD Parser Pack (`cad_upload`)
* **Purpose**: Unlocks Autodesk DXF multi-stage parsing pipeline, automated layers mapping dashboard, and vectors parsing.
* **Stand-alone Pricing**: \$199/month (Available as add-on for `Starter` plan subscribers).

## 📈 Volume Pricing Slabs (Volumetric Slabs)

Tenants can expand standard plot storage limits:
* **Slab 1**: +500 Plots capacity — \$49/month
* **Slab 2**: +2,500 Plots capacity — \$149/month
* **Slab 3**: +10,000 Plots capacity — \$399/month
